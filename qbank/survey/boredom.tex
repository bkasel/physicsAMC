%% Answer the questions using a scale from 1 to 7,

%% Scoring:
%%------------------------------------------------------------
%% Below 81: You don't bore easily at all. You find amusement in the slightest thing and life is continually interesting. Lucky You~
%% 81 to 117: You get bored sometimes, but these scores reflect average levels. Try to put those down times to good use!
%% Above 117: You get bored so easily you probably dropped off while reading this. On the bright side, provided you have the right kind of boredom, creativity might come more easily to you, helping you avoid the intolerable dull life.
